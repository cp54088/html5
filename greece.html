Travel Two: Greece


Oia

This town was right on the coast and absolutely stunning. The water is a striking shade of blue and there are cute white houses all throughout the town. Its a perfect place to get a ton of pictures. The houses built into the cliff make the whole place really unique.



Parthenon

Of course we had to go see the Parthenon! This symbol of Greece was unbelievable to see in person. The famous landmark looks just like it does in the all photos, except it looks MUCH bigger in person. Go see for yourself.

For information on the Parthenon, click here.



Petani Beach

The entire country of Greece has such beautiful beaches and ocean views. We had to spend time admiring the beach from up close. The water is so clear no matter where you are. There is nothing like relaxing on a beach, especially if it's a beach in Greece.



Athens

This capital city is filled with tons of historical landmarks and museums that you could visit for days. Even so, its amazing to just walk through the city streets and enjoy the atmosphere. There's a completely different feel you get while strolling through these streets.



Melissani Cave

There is nothing like sitting in a boat and slowly making your way through this cave. Melissani Lake, or Melissani cave is famous for just how gorgeous it is. We agreed with the common opinion. The hue of the water and the sunlight shining in makes you feel like you're in a whole new world.
